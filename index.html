Frequently Asked Questions

Is this YouTube downloader free to use?

Yes, our YouTube downloader is completely free to use. You can download unlimited videos without any registration or payment required.

What video qualities are supported?

We support all available qualities from 144p to 4K (2160p), including 240p, 360p, 480p, 720p, and 1080p. The available qualities depend on the original video.

Can I download entire playlists?

You can download entire YouTube playlists with a single click. Our tool will process all videos in the playlist and provide download links for each.

Is it legal to download YouTube videos?

Downloading videos for personal use is generally acceptable, but you should respect copyright laws and YouTube's terms of service. Only download content you have permission to use.

What is the rendering process and why is it needed?

YouTube stores high-quality videos as separate video and audio streams. Our rendering process combines these streams to create the final video file. This is required for qualities above 720p to ensure optimal playback.

How long does the rendering process take?

Rendering time varies based on video quality and length. Typically, 720p videos take 1-2 minutes, 1080p takes 2-4 minutes, and 4K/8K videos can take 5-8 minutes to process completely.

Are there any download limits or restrictions?

Our service is free with reasonable usage limits. You can download up to 50 videos per day and 10 concurrent downloads. For batch downloads, the limit is 20 URLs per batch to ensure optimal performance.

Can I download copyrighted content?

No, downloading copyrighted content without permission is prohibited. Our service is intended for personal use, educational content, Creative Commons videos, and content you own or have explicit permission to download.

What happens if my batch download fails?

If a batch download encounters errors, successfully processed videos will still be available for download. Failed videos will be listed with error details, and you can retry them individually or in a new batch.

Do you store downloaded videos on your servers?

No, we don't store any downloaded content on our servers. Videos are processed in real-time and delivered directly to your device. All temporary processing files are automatically deleted after download completion.
